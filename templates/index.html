New Game

Mode

How to use

1

Enter your word length under New Game, choose a mode, and hit Start.

2

The app suggests a word - play it in your Wordle game.

3

Type the colors Wordle showed you into the text box (see below), press Enter, and repeat!

How to enter the colors

R

Green (right spot) → type the letter UPPERCASE

a

Yellow (wrong spot) → type the letter lowercase

-

Gray (not in word) → type a dash -

Example

The app suggests CRANE, Wordle shows:

C R A N E
→

So you type:

-Ra-E

From Middle mode: already made some guesses in your game? Enter how many, then for each one type the guess and its colors separated by a space - e.g. crane -Ra-E - and the app catches up.
